My Journey

My safety journey began during my Mechanical Engineering studies, where an internship at a refinery introduced me to industrial safety. Inspired by case studies such as Piper Alpha, I pursued a Diploma in Industrial Safety. I later completed NEBOSH IGC in 2020, which launched my career in a thermal power project. Since then, I’ve grown through challenging roles in India and the UK, managing large workforces, leading audits, and embracing CPD learning to stay aligned with modern safety practices.

Safety Insights

Safety has evolved from the industrial revolution era’s focus on machinery hazards to today’s complex challenges like psychosocial risks, AI ethics, and global supply chain safety. While many nations enforce strong laws, some countries still undervalue safety, treating it as secondary to productivity. True progress lies in recognising safety as a collective responsibility, ensuring that every worker returns home safe each day.
