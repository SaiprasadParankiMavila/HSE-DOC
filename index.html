Safety Insights

Growth of safety

Safety evolved from the Industrial Revolution‚Äôs minimal protections to today‚Äôs integrated systems. Organisations now see safety as an investment, not just a cost.

Hazards then vs now

  • Then: poor ventilation, unsafe machinery, fire, child labour, no PPE.
  • Now: chemical exposure, ergonomics, stress, digital fatigue, AI-related risks.
