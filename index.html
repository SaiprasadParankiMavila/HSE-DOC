Safety Insights

Short, useful principles

  • People over paperwork: forms don’t save lives—habits do.
  • Risk is dynamic: re-check at handovers, shift changes, and when scope changes.
  • Permission to stop work: everyone should feel safe to pause when unsure.
  • Weak signals matter: near-misses and small deviations are early warnings.
  • Design out hazards: eliminate before PPE. Simplicity beats complexity.
  • Learn fast, share fast: small debriefs prevent big incidents.
  • Respect local reality: rules travel poorly—adapt the method, keep the standard.
