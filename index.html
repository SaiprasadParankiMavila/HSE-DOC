My Journey

My safety journey began during my Mechanical Engineering studies, where an internship at a refinery introduced me to industrial safety. Inspired by case studies such as Piper Alpha, I pursued a Diploma in Industrial Safety. I later completed NEBOSH IGC in 2020, which launched my career in a thermal power project. Since then, I’ve grown through challenging roles in India and the UK, managing large workforces, leading audits, and embracing CPD learning to stay aligned with modern safety practices.

Safety Insights

Safety is not just compliance — it’s culture. From the Industrial Revolution, where unguarded machines and poor ventilation caused tragedies, to the Modern Era, where challenges include psychosocial stress, climate-related risks, and AI-driven work, safety has evolved as a universal responsibility. Nations that lag behind must understand: safety is not a cost, but an investment in people and progress.

Future Goals

My next milestones include NEBOSH IDip, a Master’s in Risk Analysis, achieving CMIOSH, CSP, and RSP certifications, and ultimately contributing at the International Labour Organization (ILO) to promote global worker safety.
