Safety Insights

Top 5 Hazards in Construction Sites

Falls, struck-by objects, electrical hazards, scaffold safety, and improper PPE use remain the most common causes of accidents. A strong induction and daily inspection culture can significantly reduce risks.

What Auditors Look for in ISO 45001

Auditors focus on evidence of risk assessments, worker participation, leadership commitment, and continual improvement. Clear documentation and practical implementation are equally important.
